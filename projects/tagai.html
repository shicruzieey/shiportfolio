TAGAI Visual Identity

TAGAI is a Filipino-inspired restobar in Fujieda, Japan that brings people together through drinks, music, and shared social moments. I developed a visual identity that captures a warm, neon-lit nightlife atmosphere while balancing Filipino hospitality with Japanese accessibility.

problem

TAGAI needed a brand identity that could communicate its unique Filipino-Japanese fusion concept. The brand had to feel warm and welcoming while also capturing the energy of a neon-lit nightlife venue — without losing cultural authenticity on either side.

solution

I developed a full brand system — primary logo, secondary logo, standalone icon, and marketing materials — that balances Filipino warmth with Japanese minimalism. The visual language uses bold typography and a neon-accented palette to evoke nightlife energy while staying approachable and culturally grounded.
