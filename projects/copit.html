COPIt – Competitive Thrift Shopping Mobile App

COPIt started as an academic project and later became our hackathon entry for Automate the Future, where it won 2nd Runner-Up and Best Presenter. Built with React Native and Firebase, the app gamifies secondhand shopping through a Mine-Steal-Lock mechanic and real-time bidding system to make thrifting more engaging and competitive.

problem

Secondhand shopping lacked engagement and discovery. Buyers had no motivation to explore thrift items, and sellers struggled to get visibility. Existing platforms felt transactional and dull, missing the excitement that could make thrifting a fun, competitive experience.

solution

COPIt gamifies the thrift shopping experience through a Mine-Steal-Lock mechanic and real-time bidding system. Users compete to claim items, creating urgency and excitement. The app makes secondhand shopping engaging, social, and rewarding for both buyers and sellers.
