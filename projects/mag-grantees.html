Mag-Grantees – Magsaysay Scholarship Management System

Mag-Grantees is a web-based scholarship management system designed to help manage Magsaysay scholars more efficiently. The system streamlines scholarship tracking, academic performance monitoring, document submission, and communication between scholars and administrators.

problem

The Magsaysay scholarship program relied on manual processes for tracking scholars, monitoring academic performance, and handling document submissions — making administration slow, error-prone, and difficult to scale across multiple scholars and coordinators.

solution

Mag-Grantees centralizes all scholarship management into one web platform. Scholars can submit documents, track their status, and communicate with administrators — while coordinators get a clear dashboard for monitoring performance and managing records efficiently.
